Why You Need an Expert

Recovering foreclosure surplus funds is a complex and time-sensitive process.

Time is Running Out

Each state has a strict statute of limitations on these claims. If you miss the deadline, the government keeps your money permanently. The clock is already ticking.

Complex Paperwork

The process requires specific legal documents, notarized affidavits, and proof of ownership. Navigating county bureaucracy is often overwhelming for individuals.

Avoid Costly Errors

We do this full-time. Many who try on their own get claims denied due to missing paperwork or procedural errors. We ensure your claim is filed correctly the first time.
